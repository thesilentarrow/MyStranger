MyStranger.in allows you to anonymously connect with random Nearby college students , as well as the students of your own university.

As long as the stranger's university lies within 60km of your university you can connect with them. On Video/Text both The Stranger and You are going to be anonymous to each other. Only your name & uni is going to be show but there is no way for stranger to see your profile. Thus your identity is going to remain anonymous until you add the other stranger as your friend. We value your security that's why no one can see your email except you.

- Whole MyStranger Team is also made up of college students

so please be easy on us and if you face any kind of trouble , whether there is a bug or something you don't like than please inform us at This Form or send a mail at info@mystranger.in, We are going to fix the issue as soon as we can. so please enjoy the site and share it with your friends.
